A Day at the Zoo!

Morning at River's Edge

9:00am Every zoo day, we begin by entering the south entrance and making our way to the Black Rhinocerous enclosure. I was able to see Moyo, the one-year-old Rhino. He is one of the animals that I most look forward to seeing at the St Louis Zoo!

10:00am Next, we continue our adventure in River's Edge and see the Hippopotamuses. Today, we saw several Hippopotamuses in the water and lounging. One hippopotamus was snoozing the day away.

10:30am After the hippos, we always visit the elephants. We saw one of the elephants giving herself a dust bath.
